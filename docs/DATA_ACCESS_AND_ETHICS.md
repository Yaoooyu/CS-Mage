The feature package is provided as `CS-Mage_fulldata.pkl`. It contains all
available samples and does not embed a train/validation/test split. Use the
fixed MSA five-fold split files in `../splits/msa/` and the fixed ASR
train/validation/test split files in `../splits/asr/` to reproduce the reported
protocols.

## Ethics and Data Use

CS-Mage includes publicly available online videos and recordings collected through field interviews. Publicly accessible videos were downloaded and clipped solely for scientific research, and explicit permission was obtained from the relevant influencers or content creators for the use of their videos. All participants involved in field interviews were informed of the study purpose and provided consent before recording. For participants under 18 years of age, consent was additionally obtained from their parents or legal guardians. The dataset is intended exclusively for non-commercial scientific research and educational purposes and must not be used for identity recognition, surveillance, commercial exploitation, or other potentially harmful applications.
